What's a hashflag?

Hashflags appear along with some twitter hashtags like this one. Twitter uses them to celebrate some hashtags because all hashtags are equal, but some hashtags are more equal than others and they get their own emoji on Twitter.


You can search for all hashtags which have active hashflags on this website. Thanks for visiting!
